ABOUT DARAJA

A Career Accelerator and Community for Women

With the rapid pace of technological change and innovation and frequent promotion and performance review cycles, we need regular career acceleration: technical training, personal growth and leadership development. Daraja aims to make it easy to accelerate your career. Through Daraja, you can learn new technical skills, build your professional skills, and gain access to a network for advice, support and job opportunities. In addition to serving individuals, Daraja is also for employers, who can use Daraja to access high-quality talent and support their own employee’s career development.

Daraja provides three main programs for individuals and businesses:
  • Career Accelerator
  • Career Community
  • Job Marketplace
Mission

Daraja is a career accelerator and community providing technical upskilling, professional development, and career support to empower women to get into and get ahead in tech

Vision

Our vision is to prepare women for this future of work, so that they have access to meaningful work and advance their careers.

Core Values

  • Community
  • Do the Work, Own it
  • Be generous
  • Embrace failure to learn quickly
  • Be proud of yourself
